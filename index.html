About REPS

The Real Estate and Property Symposium is organized by The Marketplace Alliance of This Present House in partnership with ThinkMint Nigeria

The Theme of the 2021 inaugural Real Estate and Property Symposium (REPS) is
“Real Estate: An Economic Opportunity and Game-Changer”

This year’s event will focus on showing buyers, investors and consumers how to build real estate portfolios, acquire their first, second or third homes, access mortgage finance and become investors in this fast evolving sector. This will also give developers, agents and realtors an opportunity to generate hundreds of sales leads, build their brand and showcase their products, assets, land, and housing units to potential buyers for acquisition.

Led by expert speakers and practitioners, the Symposium will review current industry macro-trends and understand the contribution of the real estate sector to broader economic development

It will empower aspirational homeowners on options for owning or purchasing properties or investing in real estate as an asset class that can contribute to sustainable wealth creation

It will highlight emerging investment opportunities in the local and international real estate sector

It will provide business development opportunities for developers, realtors, agents and prospective investors

Panel Sessions

Plenary 1: New Drivers of Growth in Real Estate and Property Development

This Plenary will examine the key opportunities, challenges and dynamics in the real estate and property market, with a focus on supply side and development opportunities, examining pricing trends, new location hotspots, hospitality assets, and emerging technology in the sector that is positioning it for growth. It will also look at entrepreneurial and investment opportunities in the sector, and the different market segments e.g. luxury, mid-market, affordable, as well as commercial and residential trends, and the extent to which the current policy and regulatory environment in the industry is conducive to growth.

Plenary 2: The Path to Homeownership: Acquisition and Investment Options

This sector will examine available options, tips and strategies for purchasing and owning your first home. It will provide a broad overview on how Nigeria’s mortgage finance sector is evolving, and examine new financing trends for home acquisition for owner-occupiers and for investors seeking it as a path to build sustainable wealth. Panelists will also touch on the risks and rewards involved in home acquisition and investment, and the current and emerging investment opportunities that exist both locally and overseas. Other modes of investing in real estate such as Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITS), Real Estate funds, building a property portfolio, rent-to-own, time-share, special purpose vehicles etc. will also be explored.
